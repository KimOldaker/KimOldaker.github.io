3. Connecting to VPN#

Setting up a Virtual Private Network (VPN) on an Android device is a great way to ensure that your data is securely transmitted over a public Wi-Fi connection. For Android 4.4 and below, it is easy to set up a fast, secure VPN. Here are the steps to do it:

Firstly, open your Android phone’s Settings and select Wireless & Networks. From the list, look for VPN and tap the entry to open a page with a “+” sign at the top right corner of the page. Now enter the necessary details required for setting up a VPN connection, including the server name, type, username and password.

After that, you will need to select a type of VPN you want to use. There are two types of VPNs available - PPTP and L2TP/IPSec. Select the VPN that’s best for your connection.

Once the type is selected, tap the “Save” button at the top right corner of the page. This will add the VPN profile to your Android device. To connect, select the newly added profile and enter your username and password. Finally, tap the “Connect” button and you will be securely connected to the VPN.

With these easy steps, you can easily set up a fast and secure VPN connection on Android 4.4 and below.
